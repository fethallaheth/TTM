# Source Map

| Variable Group | Variables | Source Type | Thesis Treatment |
|---|---|---|---|
| Apple accounting data | cash, marketable securities, debt, net income, shareholders' equity, tax rate | Apple Form 10-K | Observed company data |
| Market inputs | risk-free rate, beta, market risk premium, market capitalization, legacy cost of debt | Public market / valuation sources | Market inputs |
| Scenario assumptions | adoption levels, haircuts, buffer ratios, collateral efficiency spread, technology risk premium, reinvestment return | Author calibration | Scenario assumptions |
| Model outputs | usable collateral, additional usable collateral, capital liberated, WACC change, ROE change | Python calculations | Simulation results |

Note: Calibrated assumptions should not be presented as directly observed Apple data.
